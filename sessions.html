About us

The Badcore Horns are a Devon based horn section for hire. Simon Dobson (trumpet, flugelhorn), Dan Hillman (saxophones, clarinet, flute, EWI), Chris Spreadbury (Trombones) and Ben Plocki (bass and additonal saxophones) make up the core of the group and have worked together for a number of years, performing and recording for a swathe of talented artists.

Over that time, Badcore have lent out their crew as a unit and individually for recording sessions, backing bands and dep work. They've worked at studios such as Real World and the Radio 1 Live Lounge and appeared with acts at various festivals including Glastonbury, Boardmasters and Kendal Calling.

Each member is an experienced professional musician in their own right, coming together to create an unstoppable section capable of producing a huge, tight sound and clinically crisp recordings along with a great live performance.

Sessions/Live

The Badcore Horns are available for live shows and studio recording on location or at our own studios. We can supply all your horn related demands - from huge thundering ensembles to slick solos, trumpet and trombone, clarinet, flute and a whole suite of saxes.

For live performances, just send us the tracks/arrangements/guides and we'll do the rest. We're also happy to come and rehearse with you to make sure you get the tightest show possible.

When it comes to recording some fat brass for your records we can be pretty versatile. We work closely with producer Peter Miles in Middle Farm Studios, Devon, giving us access to some of the best gear, rooms and production around. All of Badcore also have high quality home studios capable of recording edits/demos at short notice with no booking required.
