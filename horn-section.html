Badcore Horns are a Devon based horn section for hire.  Now working together for a number of years, they have lent out their crew as a unit and individually for recording sessions, backing bands and dep work. They've worked at studios such as Real World and the Radio 1 Live Lounge and appeared with acts at various festivals including Glastonbury, Latitude and Kendal Calling.

Each member is an experienced professional musician in their own right, coming together to create an unstoppable section capable of producing a huge, tight sound and clinically crisp recordings along with a great live performance.

WHO WE ARE

The Badcore Horns are a Devon based horn section for hire.  Now working together for a number of years, they have lent out their crew as a unit and individually for recording sessions, backing bands and dep work. They've worked at studios such as Real World and the Radio 1 Live Lounge and appeared with acts at various festivals including Glastonbury, Latitude and Kendal Calling.

Each member is an experienced professional musician in their own right, coming together to create an unstoppable section capable of producing a huge, tight sound and clinically crisp recordings along with a great live performance.

WHAT WE OFFER

  • • Studio recording on location or at our own space. From huge thundering ensembles to slick solos, trumpet and trombone, clarinet, flute and a whole suite of saxes. Tell us the sound you want and we’ll make it happen.
  • • Live performances can be rehearsed remotely or for laser precision we’ll run your set with you. Send us your tracks and we’ll tailor a 1 to 4 or more piece section of your dreams.
  • • We also offer arrangement and composition services.  Need some help finding the sickest riff? No problem, let us sort the arrangements for you. On top of that we have an in -house directory of phenomenal musicians from strings to percussion ready to work on your tracks. If you need something else just ask us.
